Our Services

Click on any of the services below to learn more about what we offer:

  • + Road Accident Fund Claims

    • We assist clients in claiming compensation from the Road Accident Fund for injuries or loss of income resulting from motor vehicle accidents.

  • + Administrators of Estate

    • We provide comprehensive estate administration services, including handling deceased estates and drafting wills to ensure your assets are managed according to your wishes.

  • + Divorce

    • Our firm offers legal advice and representation in divorce proceedings, ensuring that your rights are protected and that the process is as smooth as possible.

  • + Criminal Law

    • We provide robust defense services for individuals facing criminal charges, working diligently to protect your rights and achieve the best possible outcome.

  • + Civil Work Contracts

    • Our legal team assists in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating civil contracts to ensure your interests are safeguarded in any agreement you enter.

  • + Eviction

  • + Medical Negligence

    • We represent clients in medical negligence cases, fighting for the compensation you deserve for any harm caused by medical professionals' errors.
